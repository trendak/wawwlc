1st Warsaw International Workshop on
Lung Cancer Diagnostics WAWWLC

MAY 15th , 2019

workshops

1st Warsaw International Workshop on Lung Cancer Diagnostics aims to develop skills and knowledge in the field of lung cancer diagnostics.

The participants will have an opportunity to get acquainted with the current data on epidemiology, diagnosis and treatment of lung cancer. Our staff will present online procedures using modern diagnostic equipment. During the workshop, emphasis will be given to the use of EBUS (conventional EBUS-TBNA and EUS), rigid bronchoscopy, combined therapeutic techniques and thoracoscopy.
